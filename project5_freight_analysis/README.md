# Freight Cost & Carrier Performance Analysis

## Objective
Analyze shipping and freight costs by carrier, warehouse, and sales channel to evaluate logistics efficiency and measure freight expense as a percentage of revenue.

## Tables Used
- shipments
- orders
- order_items

## Key Metrics
- Freight Cost
- Average Shipping Cost per Order
- Freight % of Revenue = Shipping Cost / Revenue
- Shipment Count
- Cost by Carrier
- Monthly Freight Trend

## SQL Skills Demonstrated
- JOIN
- GROUP BY
- CTEs
- DATE_TRUNC
- COALESCE and NULLIF

## Dashboard Visuals
1. Total Freight Cost KPI
2. Freight % of Revenue KPI
3. Freight Cost by Carrier
4. Average Shipping Cost by Carrier
5. Shipment Count by Carrier
6. Monthly Freight Cost Trend
7. Freight % by Sales Channel
8. Highest-Cost Shipments Table

## Key Findings
- Freight as a percentage of revenue fluctuated over time and increased during certain periods.
- Some channels carried higher shipping costs relative to revenue.

## Recommendations
1. Review carrier contracts and negotiate rates with high-cost providers.
2. Monitor freight % of revenue as a recurring KPI.
3. Investigate unusually expensive shipments.
4. Optimize packaging and shipping methods to reduce costs.
5. Compare carrier performance regularly to improve logistics efficiency.
